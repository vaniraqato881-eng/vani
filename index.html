Luxury by the Lagoon

about Us

Fiji Marriott Resort Momi Bay, located on Viti Levu's western coast, is a premier luxury destination known for being the only resort on the Fiji mainland with iconic overwater bungalows. Situated about 45 minutes from Nadi Airport, it features a stunning man-made lagoon, three pools (including an adults-only infinity pool), diverse dining options, and an extensive, award-winning spa.
